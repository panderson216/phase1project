Charles Darwin

Publisher of On the Origin of Species

Life and Accomplishments of Charles Darwin:

  • 1809 - Darwin is born in Shrewsbury, England

  • 1818 - Attends Shrewsbury boarding school after the death of his mother.

  • 1825 - Begins studying medicine at University of Edinburgh.

  • 1831 - Darwin joins the HMS Beagle Voyage that lasts 5 years and takes him around the world.

  • 1833 - While on his voyage, Darwin discovers two new human civilizations.

  • 1839 - Darwin marries his 1st cousin, Emma Wedgwood and they have 10 children together. Three died in childhood.

  • 1859 - His most known book, On the Origin of Species, is published. This outlines his theory of evolution.

  • 1871 - Darwin's second book on evolutionary theory, The Descent of Man is published.

  • 1881 - Darwin publishes his last book, The Formation of Vegetable Mould through the Actions of Worms.

  • 1882 - Dies at the age of 73.
